NON CONJUGATION ACTIVE VERBS:

- Vowel final -
ex: va "to go"
Basic:                  --      va
First & Third person:   --      va
Second person:          -t      vat
Present Participle:     -ein    vein
Past Participle:        -nte    vante     
Middle Past:            es-te   es vante
Full Past:              er-nte  er vante
Future:                 - en    va en

- Consonant final (voiceless) -
ex: theis "to be quiet"
Basic:                  --      theis
First & Third Person:   -e      theise 
Second Person:          -et     theiset
Present Participle:     -en     theisen
Past Participle:        -te     theiste
Middle Past:            eif-te  eif theiste
Full Past:              at-te   at theiste
Future:                 - en    theis en

- Consonant final (voiced) -
ex: rein "to run"       
Basic:                  --      rein
First & Third Person:   -e      reine
Second Person:          -et     reinet
Present Participle:     -en     reinen
Past Participle:        -te     reinte
Middle Past:            es-te   es reinte
Full Past:              er-te   er reinte        
Future:                 - en    rein en
